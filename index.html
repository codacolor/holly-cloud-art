About Holly

Holly Di Cecco is a contemporary painter whose work explores the intersection of memory, atmosphere, and light. Working primarily in oils, she captures fleeting moments of sky—the particular quality of light before a storm, the soft dissolution of clouds at dusk, the crystalline clarity of high-altitude air.

Based in Colorado, Holly draws inspiration from the dramatic skies of the American West, where the landscape and atmosphere engage in constant dialogue. Her paintings invite viewers to pause, look up, and remember the profound beauty that unfolds above us every day.

Her work is held in private collections across North America, Europe, and Asia.
